What's This All About?

Welcome to the most goofy, beginner-first, open-ended hackathon to only for the IT - Hostel Boys you've ever seen! This isn't about competition – it's about building "whatever your creativity dreams up".


Technical experience? Not required! We'll teach you everything you need to know. Just bring your curiosity and let's create something awesome together in our hostel!
